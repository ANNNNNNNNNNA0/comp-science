Traditional Art v.s. Digital Art

Traditional Art

Pros Cons
  • Forces the artists to understand the concepts better
  • Creates more natural and personal artworks and experiences
  • Allows artists to learn more about preventing mistakes
  • Artworks appear unique and differently each time due to different lightings
  • Hard or impossible to correct mistakes
  • Man made mistakes on the material
  • More time consuming

Digital Art

Pros Cons
  • Creates platform for many styles and media
  • More convenient
  • Not limited by physical features
  • Unlimited tools and colors
  • No need to purchase art supplies
  • Requires specific digital tablets
  • Computer requirement
  • Seemingly decreased value since digital art is easy to be accessed
  • Hard to defend and easy to be stolen
  • Hard to focus on one style/medium

Since tradtional art and digital art build upon each other, and the fundamentle skills are the same; both media can produce amazing artworks.
